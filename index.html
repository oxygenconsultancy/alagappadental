About Us


Dr.AL.Meenakshi Sundaram B.D.S., M.D.S., D.H.M.,
Principal - Dentist.

Dr.AL.Meenakshi Sundaram finished his Under Graduation and Post-Graduation in Pediatric Dentistry from Annamalai University. He started his practice in July 2001 and continues till date in Alagappa Multispecialty Dental Clinic. He is a private practitioner for more than 15 years. He is also an academician for more than 12 years.

Currently he is working as a professor in Annoor Dental College, Muvattupuzha, and Kerala. He is a guide for Post-Graduation students.

He is an active member of Indian Dental Association (IDA) and serving as Vice President of IDA, Tamilnadu State Branch. He has been worked in various capabilities in Local and State branch. He is currently the Secretary of IDA, Kumbakonam Branch and Central Council Member of IDA, Head Office.
