ROOT CANAL

Root Canal
Dental Clinics in Kumbakonam

The goal of root canal treatment is to save the tooth by removing the infected or damaged pulp, treating any infection, and filling the empty canals with an inert material. If root canal treatment is not done, the tooth may have to be extracted.

When is root canal advised?
  • Severe toothache which is during chewing or during contact with any extreme hot or extreme cold temperature material which is usually tolerated by the mouth.
  • Constant tooth pain during mastication or chewing.
  • Darkening of the tooth
  • Swelling seen in the areas of gums around the tooth.




When tooth needs Root Canal Treatment?
Root canal treatment is needed for two main reasons
  • Infection to the pulp.
  • Irreversible damage to the pulp.

DENTAL IMPLANTS

Dental Implants
Want Great Dental Implants Fast with No Hassle?
Our Dentists can make all the Difference?

Advantages of Dental Implants over Dentures or a Bridge:
  • Esthetic : Dental implants look and feel like your own teeth!
  • Tooth-saving : Dental implants dont't sacrifice the quality of your adjacent teeth.
  • Confidence : Dental implants will allow your to once again speak and eat with comfort and confidence!
  • Reliable : The success rate of dental implants is highly predictable. They are considered and excellent option for tooth replacement.


What is Dental Implant ?

A dental implant is an artificial tooth replacement and is used to support restorations that resemble a tooth or group of teeth.

What Dental Implant Can Do?
  • Replace one or more teeth without affecting bordering teeth.
  • Support a bridge and eliminate the need for a removeable partial denture.
  • Provide support for a denture, making it more secure and comfortable.
